How Has It Impacted Our World?

This worldwide problem has affected more species and environments than we had ever known. Polar bears, the Arctic, and even coral triangles have been hit hard with their habitats shrinking to nothing.
As told from , wildlife population has been decreased at a shocking 69%, and forests that are the size of 20 football fields are getting destroyed every minute.
There has also been multiple droughts, and many ice caps being melted.

What Can We Do About It?

Something big that we can at least maintain is to not increase temperatures around the world by at least 2 degrees Celsius. If the world temperature increases by more than 2 degrees Celsius, then the entire world will be warmer than usual.
One thing we can do, is to use less energy for our homes, towns, and schools, by using solar panels. In a nutshell, solar panels convert sunlight into electrical energy that can be stored in batteries and thermal storage.
